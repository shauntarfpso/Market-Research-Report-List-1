Decongestants reduce nasal congestion by constricting blood vessels in the nasal passages. Antibiotics are utilized for bacterial infections that may accompany colds. Other remedies include nasal sprays, homeopathic treatments, and herbal supplements, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
