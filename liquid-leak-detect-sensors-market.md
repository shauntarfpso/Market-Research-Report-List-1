<p><p>The liquid leak detection sensor market is divided into two primary types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically penetrating the material or structure to directly measure liquid presence, often providing high accuracy but requiring installation effort. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection uses external sensors to monitor for leaks without damaging the surface or structure, offering ease of installation and minimal disruption. Both methods play crucial roles in enhancing safety, minimizing damage, and ensuring efficient operations across various industries.</p></p>
