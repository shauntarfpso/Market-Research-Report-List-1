<p><p>The Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) solid-state resins market includes two main types: Conventional PET and Modified PET. Conventional PET is widely used for applications such as bottles and packaging due to its excellent mechanical properties, thermal stability, and barrier characteristics. Modified PET, on the other hand, incorporates additives or blends to enhance specific properties, like improved impact resistance, flexibility, or UV stability, making it suitable for specialized applications in industries such as automotive, electronics, and textiles.</p></p>
