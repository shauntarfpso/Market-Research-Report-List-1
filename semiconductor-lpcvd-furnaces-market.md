<p><p>Semiconductor LPCVD (Low Pressure Chemical Vapor Deposition) furnaces are categorized into two main market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical LPCVD furnaces are designed for space efficiency, allowing for easier loading and cleaning, making them suitable for high-throughput applications. In contrast, horizontal LPCVD furnaces offer uniform temperature distribution and are ideal for processes requiring larger substrates. Both types serve crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to diverse production needs and varying material requirements within the industry.</p></p>
