<p><strong>The Airway Clearance Devices System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Expiratory Pressure Devices</li><li>Flutter Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airway clearance device systems market includes various types tailored for respiratory health. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices help maintain airway patency by creating resistance during exhalation, promoting mucus clearance. Flutter devices utilize a combination of positive pressure and oscillation to mobilize secretions, enhancing lung function. Other devices encompass various innovative technologies like vibratory oscillators and mechanical insufflators, designed to aid patients in clearing airways effectively. Collectively, these devices are crucial for managing conditions like cystic fibrosis and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease.</p></p>
