<p><p>The static fluid bed dryer market is characterized by two main types: continuous and batch. Continuous type dryers operate in a steady flow, allowing for uninterrupted processing of materials, which enhances efficiency and productivity. In contrast, batch type dryers process fixed quantities of materials at one time, providing greater flexibility for varied product types. Both types are utilized in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, catering to specific drying requirements based on production scales and material characteristics.</p></p>
