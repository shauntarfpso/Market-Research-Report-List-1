<p><ul><li>Raster</li><li>Vector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphics display terminal market is primarily categorized into raster and vector types. Raster displays, composed of a grid of pixels, are ideal for rendering detailed images and complex colors, making them prevalent in digital imaging and gaming. In contrast, vector displays utilize mathematical equations to represent images, allowing for scalable graphics without loss of quality, which is essential for applications like graphic design and technical illustrations. Both types serve distinct purposes in visual representation and user interaction.</p></p>
