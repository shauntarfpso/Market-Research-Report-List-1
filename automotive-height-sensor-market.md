<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive height sensor market is segmented into two main types: analog and digital. Analog height sensors utilize variable resistance to measure distance, providing continuous data that can be less precise but is simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, digital height sensors convert physical movement into digital signals, allowing for higher accuracy and integration with advanced vehicle systems. The choice between these types often depends on vehicle requirements, desired precision, and technological compatibility within the automotive control systems.</p></p>
