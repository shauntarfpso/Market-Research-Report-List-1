<p><p>The voting equipment market encompasses several key types. Optical scan paper ballot systems utilize paper ballots marked by voters, which are then scanned for counting. Direct Recording Electronic (DRE) systems allow voters to cast their votes digitally on electronic machines without paper. Ballot-Marking Devices (BMDs) provide a hybrid approach, enabling voters to mark their choices digitally while producing a paper record for verification. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the accessibility, accuracy, and security of the electoral process.</p></p>
