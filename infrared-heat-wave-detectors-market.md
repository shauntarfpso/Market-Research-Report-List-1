<p><p>The infrared heat wave detectors market is segmented into three types based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR). SWIR detectors operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range, suitable for imaging in low-light conditions. MWIR detectors, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are ideal for thermal imaging and surveillance. LWIR detectors, covering 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in heat detection and are widely used in thermal cameras and industrial applications.</p></p>
