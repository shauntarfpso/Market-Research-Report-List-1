<p><p>RISC-V Cores are categorized into two primary market types: RV32 and RV64. RV64, on the other hand, represents 64-bit architecture, suitable for high-performance computing, data-intensive applications, and server environments. This distinction allows developers to choose the appropriate core based on performance needs and resource constraints while benefiting from the flexibility and scalability of the RISC-V ecosystem.</p></p>
