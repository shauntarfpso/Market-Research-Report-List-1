<p><p>Standard Recovery Diode Rectifiers are semiconductor devices designed to convert alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC) in various applications. Their reliability and efficiency in power management make them popular in power supplies, motor drives, and automotive applications.</p><p>The Standard Recovery Diode Rectifiers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.90% during the forecast period.
