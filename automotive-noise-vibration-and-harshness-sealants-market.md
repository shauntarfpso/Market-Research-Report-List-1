<p><p>The Automotive Noise, Vibration, and Harshness (NVH) Sealants market is divided into two main types: bonded and non-bonded. Bonded sealants are designed to create a strong adhesion between surfaces, effectively minimizing noise and vibrations by forming a solid barrier. In contrast, non-bonded sealants provide temporary dampening effects, often used in applications where ease of removal or repositioning is needed. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle comfort and performance by addressing NVH issues.</p></p>
